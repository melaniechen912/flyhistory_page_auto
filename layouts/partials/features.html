Automatic Tracking

FlyHistory automatically records flights that users view, eliminating the need for manual input or data entry.

History Management

Users can easily access and manage their flight history directly within the extension interface.

Flight Details

FlyHistory provides detailed information about tracked flights, including departure and arrival times, airlines, and more.

Customization

Users can customize their tracking preferences and settings to suit their individual needs and preferences.

User-Friendly Interface

FlyHistory offers an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it simple for users to navigate and utilize the extension.
